Snapshot is a URL-capture website rather like Reddit or Digg that allows users to submit their favorite websites, take pictures of them, and then let the world vote on them. View other users' submitted websites, vote on them, and see the most interesting links of the day!

Snapshot is a website by Varun Ravishankar and Jervis Muindi built on top of Apache, Perl, C++, and SQLite 3.
